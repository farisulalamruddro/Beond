Activities

Underwater sports

Snorkel or dive the tranquil clear waters and colourful coral gardens of the islands. Swim with manta rays, meet friendly hawksbill turtles and spot a whale shark lurking in the shallows.

Yachting, boating and fishing

Soak up the setting – or rising – sun aboard a luxury yacht. Set sail to pristine uninhabited islands and sandbanks for a day in your own private paradise. Climb aboard a dhoni and explore the best spots for deep-sea and big game fishing.

Wellness

From Ayurveda to medical spa, discover a holistic range of transformative wellness treatments on offer in the Maldives’ world-renowned spas.
